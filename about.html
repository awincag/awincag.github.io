About

.

I'm a self-taught designer and a front-end developer. I currently lead the design team over at West Agile Labs, a digital design and development agency based in San Francisco.

I'm a huge fan of typography, photography, seamless user experiences and decadent (borderline hedonistic) breakfast tacos.

A Whole History of Me

Born and raised in lovely South Bay, California.

I went to an all-boys high school, Bellarmine Prep, where I ran cross country and track and field. I received my undergrad degree in Political Science at California Polytechnic University, San Luis Obispo. I also started the Cal Poly Distance Club with my best friend Mike, which now has 100+ Members!

I started my career track in San Francisco (haven't left... yet), working product management and project management at a small 3 person startup, Ten Dollar Wholesale for close to a year. We did great while I was there, increasing revenue by 120% and product selection from 600 to 1200. It was a great start, but not a great fit for me in the end.

Next, I found a small development agency at the time (across the hall -- both companies were sharing space) needed a project / product manager who had the ability to do some wireframing. This was West Agile Labs, which is where I am today.

Initially doign project management, I was managing anywhere from 6-8 projects/teams at any given time. Most teams consistented of 2-3 developers and one designer. It be came very evident though that clients needed product thought & management. While most people interact with product, most people completely suck at it. Example: everyone knows how to drive a car, some people now how to fix it. Most people wouldn't want to build their own car and drive it to work everyday. Anyways, doing a combination of product management, where I was introduced to wireframing and product design, and project management, it was clear that my skillsets lied in user experience design. For awhile, I was managing (both product and project) and designing anywhere from 4-5 projects at a time.

I quickly became insanely passionate about the entire creative & design process. Reverse engineering & designing everything I could get my hands on, reading every design team blog, getting all the books everyone says to buy on medium. I slowly transitioned from product manager to full-stack product designer.

By now, I've worked on over 50+ projects & products. My projects have ranged from emerging, early-stage startups getting their prototype out the door to two Fortune 500 companies. I've sourced and hired 4 designers and currently manage 3 of them. With legacy projects, I still oversee product development completely, one with a team of 8 developments. I've had the opportunity to lead art direction on our company rebrand as well as do all of the front-end development for our new company website.

I specialize in interaction design, but have the ability to work on storyboarding, branding (mainly typography), visual design, animation design, front-end development and product management. I currently lead our awesome design team of 5. I champion the value and need for design on our all of our products to both the clients and the rest of the West Agile team. I aim to bridge the gap between development and all visual communication. I love what I do.

Occasionally, I'll do freelance work on the side. Scroll down to learn more about my philosophy and getting in contact.

I also make some really good breakfast tacos. Seriously, this shit is decandant and almost hedonistic. Check out the website here to learn more about them and how you can get a bite yourself.
