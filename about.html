About Me

About Me
Hey there. Welcome to my abode.
Fun Fact
I spent 4 months roadtripping across the country, driving 13,702 miles and staying in 32 cities. I met a dude named Herman in Marfa, TX who thought it'd be a good idea to drive my car to 132mph.
I’m Alex, a digital design director, currently freelancing in New York. I’m also on a quest to make the world’s best breakfast tacos.

I primarily focus on product and interaction design for companies like Harley Davidson, Fidelity, Natera, Octane AI and ShearShare. I work in a collaborative and highly iterative fashion emphasizing building and testing prototypes with users.

I started my craeer at West Agile Labs. I was there for 3 years, where I built out and oversaw the design and product teams. I led our design team of five, pitched clients, managed resources, developed the West Agile brand and worked on over 45+ products, such as Natera, SpikeBee, Nvolv, Eventyoda, Octane AI, and ShearShare, which won Tech.Co’s startup of the year.

After West Agile, I worked for Harley Davidson for the better part of 2017. During that time, the product team focused on re-designing their existing ride planning tool. For the first half of the engagement, I had the opportunity to work out of Pivotal Labs with talented team there. Some of the designers I had the privilege to work with were Richard Enlow, Prianka Rayamajhi and David Ghent.

I had moved to NYC after the Harley Davidson engagement where I started working over with SapientRazorfish helping craft and build Marcel, the AI powered assistant. I'm currently helping redefine their agency workflow as well define the UX framework for a conversational interface.

In the middle of 2018, I partnered again with a one of the most talented product people I know, Rathan Haran, where we helped transform Baby Noggin's product. We brought to the table, lean and agile methdologies to help better support the Baby Noggin ecosystem, focusing on iterative prototyping and research. We are currently helping doctors and teachers better screen children.

Born and raised in lovely South Bay, California. I went to BCP and received my undergrad degree in Political Science at Cal Poly, SLO, where I ran cross-country and track at both. I also started the Cal Poly Distance Club with my best friend Mike

On the side, I try to keep busy with photography, making music, and lots of side projects. The two big ones I’m working on right now are The Underground Symphony and Dodo. My biggest passion outside of the world of design is some really good fucking breakfast tacos. Seriously, they’re decadent. Check out the website here to learn more about them and how you can get a bite yourself.

Experience
Product Design Lead Marcel at Publicis January 2018 – Present

Marcel is an AI enabled assistant for employees of the Publicis Groupe, where I currently oversee the entire digital ecosystem. This includes the both mobile apps content and editorial tools, content management and web products. I oversee a talented team of 5 UX designers and support in managing the 5 person VD team. I also manage the end to end workflow between design and development.

Head of Design Baby Noggin June 2018 – Present

I oversee all things design for Baby Noggin, a startup aimed at providing tools and resources to Doctors and Parents with young childs. I work on a small team of 6 aimed at optimizing developmental screening workflows. I spend much of my time in research understanding child development and validating design concepts. We are currently exploring solutions to connect the bridge between low-income families, schools and doctors. These are some of the hardest problems I've worked on my career to date.

Design Lead Harley Davidson February 2017 – November 2017

Helped re-designed the Harley-Davidson Ride planner for iOS, Android and web, a core digital product and service for the company. The first third of the engagement was in conjunction with the talented team over at Pivotal Labs. After buliding out intitial concepts and prototypes on iOS, I worked closely with Gigster throughout the rest of 2017 where I lead and managed 4 product designers to continue pushing iOS as well as the overhaul on Android and web. The ride preview we came up with will forever be my most satisfying design yet.

Design Lead West Agile Labs March 2014 – February 2017

My first agency and second job. I started out as a QA analyst trying to break products all day long. I quickly picked up product and started managing projects & product. After our Lead Designer quit sometime through, I had an opportunity to teach myself design and take up the mantle. I started the design team there and ended up hiring and mentoring 5 designers. I help transition West Agile Labs from an offershore development shop to a full integrated product design studio.

Over my tenure there, we worked on over 60+ products. I lead design a for a large chunk of those products. I managed and maintained the design pipeline, where I pitched almost all new clients. I worked with the founding partners to grab our team from 5 people to 28. Additionally, we grew from 1 devleopment partner to 6 by the time I had left.
