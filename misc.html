West Agile Labs is a SF-based digital design and development agency. Helps Lead art direction along with the awesome Jackie Cruickshank for the new brand. Additionally, did all of the front-end development for the website. Launching very soon.

Underground Symphony

A platform to discover and profile the artists of New York's subway system. Using Vogue & Rolling Stones as influences, the idea here is to present unheard of artists in over-the-top portrait series. In conjunction with my friend and very talent photographer, Nate.
