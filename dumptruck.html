While the gig economy has risen in new areas, it's been strong in markets such as big-rig trucking and dumptrucking. Bucky's Hauling LLC is a network of trucking comapnies and suppliers fulfilling orders on an on-demand basis. They were looking for a transition from excel spreadsheets to the digital world to better manage and scale their business. They partnered with Gigster and their freelance network to take them into the digital age.

I worked closely with product manager, Rathan Haran, and art director, Brian Basher, to create a new digital experience for Bucky's Hauling. We focused on streamlining the entire order journey and automating complex workflows to help them scale Bucky's Hauling' business. There was a simple goal: get rid of the paper tickets needed at every stage of the trucking process. We designed a platform for dispatchers, customers, trucking companies and truck drivers to manage their respective needs and eliminate the tremendous paper trail. The product is currently in the development phase and will be launching end of Summer 2017. Once it launches, it'll be known as Dumptruck.

Dispatch Ecosystem

At the heart of the new digital experience, there needed to be a robust admin system to manage every aspect of every order. We designed an extensive platform to manage customers, new and incoming orders, current loads, trucking companies, suppliers and materials. Each piece revolved around the life-cycle of the order and it's respective loads.
