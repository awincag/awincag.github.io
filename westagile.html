Overview

I joined West Agile in March of 2014. There was only 3 full time employees, 1 part-time, 1 other intern and 2 overseas partners. Today, we have 26 full-time employees, 6 partners and over 250 projects completed.

When I started, we had a somewhat of a throw-away brand, with a logo from 99 designs that was created back in 2012. While I have nothing against 99 designs, you are probably not going to get a timeless logo for $499. Maybe, but probably not.

2015 was a big year for us as we expanded the team and worked on large scale projects, some of which for fortune 500 companies. However, the brand that we had wasn't keeping up. We contracted out to "Few of Many", a branding agency out in Oakland to re-do the logo and color. The re-brand was wrapped up early 2016.

Unfortunately, things didn't work out as we had hoped and the relationship between the two agencies slowly dwindled. That left the remaining deisgn team to pick up where they had left off.

With an upcoming event in late February that set our deadline, we quickly got to work. Unfortunately, the brand and website that we had pushed out at the time was rushed and didn't reflect who we were as company and team. We realized that we were only about 50% of where we knew we were. Jackie and myself, slowly started chipping away, evolving the brand and the voice of the company over the coming months to help upgrade us to where we knew we were.

Being a small team of 5, we juggled important work while still fine tuning the visual identity of who we are. Come end of July, we were able to make a big push and see progress in the direction we had hoped. From there, we were swamped under client work until only relatively recently, here in November. The following screens are a reflection of that latest work. This iteration of the website should be up in the next few weeks.
